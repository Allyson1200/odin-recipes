Sugar skull cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Dixie Crystals Extra Fine Granulated Sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour*
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, very soft
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Sprinkles
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar

Steps

  1. *Spoon & Sweep method: Use a spoon to fill measuring cup
    with flour until required amount is obtained. Scooping measuring cup directly into flour bag
    will firmly pack flour resulting in too much flour required for recipe.

  2. Preheat oven to 350Â°F

  3. Beat butter until very light and creamy. Add sugar and continue mixing
    until light and fluffy. Add egg, vanilla, and salt and beat until very well combined.
  4. Sift flour, baking soda and cream of tartar
    together and add in one step to above. Mix until dough forms. Do not over mix.

  5. Press dough
    about 1/2-inch thick onto a plastic food film lined cookie sheet and place
    in the refrigerator for 30-60 m

  6. Roll dough on evenly sugared kitchen towel 1/4-inch thick. Using a skull shaped cookie
    cutter, cut out and place on parchment or lightly buttered cookie sheets.

  7. Place in oven and bake until very pale and edges are very light golden, about 9 minutes

  8. Prepare royal icing while cookies cool. Tint/color
    royal icing based on your cookie decoration and decorate with mini M&Ms,chocolate covered sunflower seeds, mini
    chocolate chips, and sprinkles.
